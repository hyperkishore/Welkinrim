Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, customisation, and support.

Our standard MOQ is 10 units for catalogue motors. For custom winding configurations or defence-grade modifications, MOQ starts at 25 units. We offer sample units for evaluation at standard pricing before committing to bulk orders.

Standard catalogue motors ship within 2-3 weeks. Custom configurations require 4-6 weeks. Defence-grade motors with MIL-STD certification typically take 6-8 weeks. We maintain buffer stock of our most popular models for urgent orders.

Yes. Our engineering team can customise KV ratings, winding patterns, and thermal characteristics to match your specific mission profile. Custom windings are available on all motor series with a minimum order of 25 units.

All WelkinRim motors carry a 24-month warranty covering manufacturing defects and premature bearing failure. Defence-grade motors include an extended 36-month warranty. We also offer optional extended warranty and field-support packages for fleet operators.

Our motors range from IP54 (micro series) to IP68 (defence and industrial series). IP68-rated motors are fully submersible and tested at 2m depth for 30 minutes. Agriculture series motors feature IP65 with additional chemical-resistant coatings.
