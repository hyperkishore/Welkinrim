Our Certifications

From battlefield extremes to airborne systems, our certifications prove that WelkinRim motors perform where failure is not an option.

Military Standard

MIL-STD-810H

Environmental Engineering Considerations and Laboratory Tests

Our motors are tested and certified to withstand the full spectrum of environmental extremes defined by MIL-STD-810H, including high and low temperature cycling, humidity exposure, mechanical vibration, operational shock, and sand and dust ingress. This standard ensures reliable performance in the harshest field conditions encountered by defence and tactical drone platforms.

What this means for you

Deploy with confidence in desert heat, arctic cold, tropical humidity, high-vibration airframes, and dusty operational theatres without motor degradation.

Military Standard

MIL-STD-461G

Requirements for the Control of Electromagnetic Interference

WelkinRim motors comply with MIL-STD-461G electromagnetic interference and electromagnetic compatibility requirements. This certification verifies that our motors neither emit harmful electromagnetic radiation nor are susceptible to interference from external sources, ensuring clean operation alongside sensitive avionics, communications gear, and sensor payloads in defence environments.

What this means for you

Operate alongside sensitive communications, ISR payloads, and electronic warfare systems without signal degradation or electromagnetic interference.

Ingress Protection

IP67 Rating

Dust Tight + Temporary Water Immersion

IP67-rated WelkinRim motors provide complete protection against dust ingress (level 6) and are tested to withstand temporary immersion in water up to 1 metre depth for 30 minutes (level 7). This rating ensures full operational capability in rain, mud, coastal spray, and environments where particulate contamination is a constant threat.

What this means for you

Fly confidently through rain, coastal conditions, and dusty environments. Emergency water landings and washdown cleaning will not compromise motor integrity.

Ingress Protection

IP68 Rating

Dust Tight + Continuous Water Submersion

Select WelkinRim motor configurations achieve IP68 certification, providing complete dust-tight sealing and the ability to withstand continuous submersion under pressure beyond 1 metre depth. This is the highest standard of ingress protection, designed for underwater drone applications, amphibious operations, and missions requiring sustained exposure to water.

What this means for you

Enable amphibious drone platforms and underwater applications. Motors maintain full performance during prolonged submersion and extreme wet-environment operations.

Aerospace Standard

DO-160G

Environmental Conditions and Test Procedures for Airborne Equipment

DO-160G certification confirms that WelkinRim motors meet the rigorous environmental test procedures required for airborne equipment. This covers altitude performance, temperature variation, vibration and shock, power input quality, voltage spikes, and electromagnetic compatibility specific to aviation-grade components used in manned and unmanned aerial systems.

What this means for you

Integrate WelkinRim motors into certified airborne platforms with confidence. Meets the environmental qualification demanded by aviation regulators and aerospace primes.

Quality Management

ISO 9001:2015

Quality Management Systems - Requirements

WelkinRim maintains ISO 9001:2015 certification across all manufacturing and engineering operations. This internationally recognised standard ensures that our quality management system consistently delivers products that meet customer and regulatory requirements, with a structured framework for continuous improvement across every stage of design, production, and delivery.

What this means for you

Every motor you receive meets the same exacting quality standard. Traceable manufacturing, rigorous inspection, and a commitment to continuous process improvement ensure batch-to-batch consistency.

Export Compliance

ITAR Compliant

International Traffic in Arms Regulations

WelkinRim is registered with the U.S. Directorate of Defense Trade Controls (DDTC) and maintains full compliance with ITAR regulations governing the export and import of defence-related articles and services. Our compliance programme includes secure data handling, personnel screening, export licence management, and comprehensive recordkeeping for all controlled items.

What this means for you

Procure WelkinRim motors for defence programmes with full regulatory confidence. Our ITAR compliance ensures seamless integration into controlled supply chains and government contracts.
