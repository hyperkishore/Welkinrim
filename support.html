WelkinRim specializes in high-performance brushless motors for professional and commercial drones. Our product line includes the A2212 series for racing and FPV applications, the XR4000 series for heavy-lift platforms, and the AR2814 series for precision cinematography. All motors feature aerospace-grade construction, advanced cooling systems, and IP67 environmental protection for reliable performance in demanding conditions.

Technical

Motor selection depends on your payload weight, flight time requirements, and performance goals. Use our Motor Matchmaker tool on the homepage for personalized recommendations. Generally, the A2212 series suits racing drones (250-450mm frames), the AR2814 series is ideal for camera platforms (500-650mm frames), and the XR4000 series handles heavy-lift applications (750mm+ frames). Our technical team is available to assist with custom configurations.

For stable hovering and smooth cinematography, target a 2:1 thrust-to-weight ratio. Racing and acrobatic applications benefit from 4:1 or higher. Heavy-lift platforms should maintain at least 2.5:1 for safety margins. All WelkinRim motors include thrust tables in their datasheets showing performance across different propeller sizes and battery voltages to help you calculate exact ratios.

Each motor series is optimized for specific propeller ranges: A2212 (8-10 inch), AR2814 (11-14 inch), and XR4000 (14-18 inch). Detailed thrust and efficiency curves for each propeller size are available in the product datasheets. We recommend carbon fiber or high-quality composite propellers for best performance. Always balance propellers before installation to minimize vibration and extend motor life.

Choose an ESC rated for at least 20% above your motor's maximum current draw. For the A2212 series, use 30A+ ESCs; AR2814 requires 40A+; XR4000 needs 60A+. We recommend BLHeli_32 or BLHeli_S firmware for optimal performance. Ensure your ESC supports your desired PWM frequency (typical range: 24kHz for smoothness, 48kHz for racing). All motors are compatible with standard 3-phase ESC protocols.

Warranty Information

WelkinRim stands behind every motor with our industry-leading 3-year comprehensive warranty. We're committed to ensuring your motors deliver peak performance throughout their operational life.

Coverage Includes

  • Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
  • Bearing failures under normal operating conditions
  • Magnet demagnetization or detachment
  • Stator winding defects or insulation failures
  • Bell housing structural failures
  • Shaft alignment or concentricity issues
  • Environmental seal failures (IP67 rated components)

Warranty Exclusions

  • Damage from crashes, impacts, or foreign object ingestion
  • Operation beyond specified voltage, current, or temperature ratings
  • Wear items including bearings after normal service life (500+ flight hours)
  • Modifications, repairs, or servicing by unauthorized parties
  • Use of incompatible ESCs or propellers outside recommended specifications
  • Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect performance
  • Damage from water immersion (IP67 rating covers splashes, not submersion)
