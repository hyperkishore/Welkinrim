Optimize Your Racing Setup

Motor Timing

Adjust ESC timing settings to match motor characteristics. Higher timing increases top speed but reduces efficiency and generates more heat.

Propeller Matching

Pair high-KV motors with lower pitch props for instant acceleration, or higher pitch for top-end speed on larger tracks.

Weight Distribution

Position motors to optimize center of gravity. Even small shifts in weight placement affect cornering performance and flip characteristics.
