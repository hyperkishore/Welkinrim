How We Calculate

Annual cost includes initial motor purchase plus replacements due to failure. WelkinRim motors have an industry-leading 0.3% annual failure rate thanks to aerospace-grade bearings and IP-rated sealing.

Annual Cost = Fleet Size × Motors/Drone × Motor Cost × (1 + Failure Rate)
Savings = Current Annual Cost − WelkinRim Annual Cost
