Partner Requirements

We seek partners who share our commitment to quality, technical expertise, and customer satisfaction.

Authorized Distributor

  • Minimum initial order: 50 units
  • Quarterly minimum: 25 units
  • Established drone / UAV customer base
  • Dedicated sales staff (2+ people)
  • Local warehousing capability
  • Technical support capability
  • Active website and marketing presence

Technology Partner

  • Active R&D programme in propulsion or controls
  • Engineering team with motor / ESC expertise
  • Existing product line in adjacent technology
  • Intellectual property portfolio
  • Published research or technical publications
  • Willingness to share development roadmap

Integration Partner (OEM)

  • Annual volume commitment: 100+ units
  • Existing drone / UAS product in production
  • Integration engineering capability
  • Quality management system (ISO 9001 preferred)
  • End-customer support infrastructure
  • Product liability insurance

All Partners

  • Registered business entity
  • Minimum 2 years operating history
  • Clean regulatory and compliance record
  • Agreement to WelkinRim brand guidelines
  • Participation in quarterly business reviews
  • Completion of product certification training
