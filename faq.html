General

WelkinRim Technologies is a German-engineered drone motor manufacturer based in Munich. We specialise in brushless propulsion systems for three key sectors: Defence & Security (MIL-STD-810H certified motors), Agriculture & Farming (chemical-resistant motors for spray drones), and Commercial applications (delivery and inspection). Our motors range from micro (WR2212) to industrial (WR-D8025) with peak efficiencies up to 95%.

WelkinRim is headquartered in Munich, Germany with manufacturing and R&D facilities in the Munich Innovation Park. We ship globally to over 50 countries via DHL Express, FedEx, and UPS. See our shipping page for full details on delivery terms and territories.

WelkinRim motors offer higher peak efficiency (up to 95% vs 89-91%), superior IP ratings (IP68 vs IP45-55), wider operating temperature range (-40°C to +80°C), and full MIL-STD-810H compliance on our defence series. We also offer dedicated agriculture-grade motors with chemical-resistant coatings that competitors don't provide.

Technical

Our motors range from IP54 (micro series) to IP68 (defence and industrial series). IP68-rated motors are fully submersible, tested at 2m depth for 30 minutes. Agriculture series motors feature IP65 with additional chemical-resistant nano-coatings that withstand fertiliser and pesticide exposure.

Yes. Our engineering team can customise KV ratings, winding patterns, and thermal characteristics to match your specific mission profile. Custom windings are available on all motor series with a minimum order of 25 units and a 4-6 week lead time.

Standard commercial motors operate from -20°C to +60°C. Defence-grade (WR-D series) motors are rated from -40°C to +80°C per MIL-STD-810H environmental testing. Agriculture motors are rated -10°C to +55°C with humidity resistance up to 95% RH.

WelkinRim motors are compatible with all standard brushless ESCs that support the required voltage and current ratings. We recommend pairing with Hobbywing, T-Motor, or APD ESCs for optimal performance. Each motor's datasheet includes recommended ESC specifications.

Our WR-A series motors are purpose-built for agricultural drones. The WR-A4014-520 and WR-A5012-400 feature IP65 chemical-resistant coatings that withstand constant exposure to fertilisers and pesticides. They enable 30+ acres per charge with precision spray control.

Warranty & Support

All WelkinRim motors carry a 24-month warranty covering manufacturing defects and premature bearing failure. Defence-grade motors include an extended 36-month warranty. We also offer optional extended warranty and field-support packages for fleet operators.

Contact our support team with your order number and a description of the issue. We'll provide an RMA number and shipping instructions. Most warranty claims are processed within 5 business days of receiving the unit. Replacement motors are shipped immediately from stock when available.

Yes. All customers receive email technical support. Partnership Programme members get dedicated engineering support with phone access and priority response times. We also provide integration guides, wiring diagrams, and ESC compatibility documentation with every motor.

Unopened motors in original packaging can be returned within 30 days for a full refund. Custom-configured motors are non-returnable. Defective motors are covered under our warranty policy. Contact support for an RMA number before shipping any returns.

For orders of 100+ units or Partnership Programme members, we offer on-site installation support and training. Our field engineers can assist with motor integration, testing, and fleet setup. Contact sales for availability and pricing.
