Standard Warranty Coverage

WelkinRim warrants all products to be free from defects in materials and workmanship under normal use and service for a period of three (3) years from the date of delivery. This warranty is provided to the original purchaser and is non-transferable.

What's Covered

  • Manufacturing Defects: Defects in materials, components, or assembly processes
  • Design Defects: Failures resulting from design flaws in standard products
  • Component Failures: Failure of bearings, windings, encoders, or other integral components under normal operation
  • Workmanship Issues: Assembly errors, improper tolerances, or quality control failures
  • Performance Issues: Failure to meet published specifications when properly installed and operated

Warranty Remedy

If a covered defect is confirmed, WelkinRim will, at its sole discretion:

  • Repair the defective product at no charge
  • Replace the defective product with an equivalent new or refurbished unit
  • Provide credit toward the purchase of a replacement product

Repaired or replaced products are warranted for the remainder of the original warranty period or 90 days from the repair/replacement date, whichever is longer.

What's Not Covered

This warranty does not cover failures or damage resulting from:

  • Improper Installation: Installation not in accordance with WelkinRim specifications and guidelines
  • Misuse or Abuse: Operation outside rated parameters, overloading, or improper application
  • Environmental Factors: Exposure to corrosive atmospheres, excessive moisture, extreme temperatures beyond specifications, or contamination
  • Unauthorized Modifications: Any alteration, repair, or modification by parties other than WelkinRim or authorized service centers
  • Normal Wear: Consumable items such as brushes, seals, or items subject to normal wear and tear
  • External Causes: Damage from accidents, natural disasters, power surges, or external electrical/mechanical interference
  • Maintenance Neglect: Failure to perform recommended maintenance or use of improper lubricants/consumables
  • Cosmetic Damage: Scratches, dents, or aesthetic issues that do not affect functionality

Important Operating Conditions

Warranty coverage requires that products are operated within specified voltage, current, speed, temperature, and environmental ratings. Operation outside these parameters voids the warranty.

Extended Warranty Options

For critical applications or enhanced peace of mind, WelkinRim offers extended warranty programs:

  • 5-Year Extended Coverage: Extends standard warranty to five years from delivery date
  • Preventive Maintenance Programs: Scheduled inspection and maintenance with priority warranty service
  • On-Site Support Plans: Available for high-value installations requiring rapid response

Contact our sales team for extended warranty pricing and eligibility. Extended warranties must be purchased at the time of product order.

Important Information

Proof of Purchase Required

All warranty claims require proof of purchase, including invoice number, purchase date, and dealer/distributor information. Serial numbers must match our manufacturing records.

Return Shipping

Customer is responsible for shipping costs to WelkinRim. If the claim is approved under warranty, WelkinRim will pay return shipping costs. If the claim is denied (non-warranty issue), customer is responsible for all shipping costs and repair charges.

Limitation of Remedies

The remedies provided in this warranty are the customer's sole and exclusive remedies. WelkinRim shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages including lost profits, downtime, or business interruption.

Geographic Coverage

This warranty is valid worldwide. However, warranty service outside the European Union may be subject to additional shipping time and may be fulfilled through authorized distributors or service centers.
